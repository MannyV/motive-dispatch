We are going to build an equivalent for OpenClaw for travel agents. 

CONTEXT OF THE USER
As a travel agent, you travel from place to place and are in touch with a large amount of travelers. They are your potential clients and customers, but also sometimes they are just people that follow you on social media. 

Your target audience spans all across the funnel from awareness to revenue. For some that are regular customers that you have already provided information (preferences, budget reality, travel history, household context) and plan travel for. For some, you were just discussing with them as they have an interest in a budget. For some of them, they are just people that follow you on social media, for instance, but you have never had a chance to work. The information you have about them varies accordingly: from strangers to close acquaientances.

Where this information lives also an issue for you. You are not necessarily super tech-savvy so you stitch together spreadsheets, DMs, email threads, forms, phycial notes, and CRM tools.

CONCEPT OF THE APP
The concept of the app is as follows. Just like Openclaw, the "app" is a persona that you grant access to (read and write differentially) everywhere you may house client information.
